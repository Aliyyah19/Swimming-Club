Introduction

On 9 July 2010, our Swimming Club MMU Cyberjaya was established and has consistently helped students improve their swimming skills. Since the establishment, we held several events and joined competitions in maintaining the interest of MMU students in swimming.

Vision

Our vision is to establish swimming club in MMU that is excellent in swimming and very good in community and society

Mission

Our mission is to provide MMU students structure learn to swim programme or to enhance their swimming skills during our member session

Achievement

We managed to secure 2 gold medals and 3 silver medals in the SSC Swimming Gala 2024.
